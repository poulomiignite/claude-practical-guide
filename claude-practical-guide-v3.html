06 — In Practice

What this looks like
in real scenarios

Example 01

Responding to a client email

Ask Claude to handle the latest client email. It reads the email, checks your CRM for history, reviews past conversations, and drafts a response that reflects everything it knows.

Example 02

Preparing for a new business call

Before a call, ask Claude to brief you on a prospect. It searches the web, pulls prior email exchanges, checks your notes — and produces a structured briefing. All from one instruction.

Example 03

End-of-week operations review

Ask Claude to pull together a summary of the week. It checks your calendar, reviews tasks, reads Slack threads, and drafts a structured summary with open items and next steps.
